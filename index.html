Grandis Moving, is a young moving company founded and growing by two best friends. The company was founded in 2021, and has already established itself in the moving service industry as reliable, highly qualified, and offering the best prices for moving service.

OUR BENEFITS

SERVICE LEVEL

THE HIGHEST LEVEL: as we work ourselves from the moment of receiving applications from the client, to the last unloaded item from the truck and placing this item at the client's delivery address. *While other companies transload things from one truck to another, forming several orders together, which puts the furniture and other customer items at risk of damage by repeated unnecessary movements, and also forces the client to increase the delivery time for many days and weeks ahead. Whereas our company, on the second order day, is already moving to the delivery address to the customer.

RAPIDITY

Our average speed in the following days is 800 - 900 miles per day. And on the day the truck arrives at the client's address, at a convenient time for the client, we begin the process of unloading and arranging all the items and furniture at the address, and assembling the furniture that was disassembled earlier. TIMING OF WORK: the speed of work unattainable for other moving companies. If it is a long distance, the first day we are busy carefully and professionally disassemble furniture, packing with special materials, loading and professional arrangement of furniture and other items in the truck.

PRICING

FIXED BID for each customer! We can say with confidence that our prices are final and STRICTLY OBTAINED by us, and can not be increased for our customers. All prices and costs of services are offered to the client, after the first consultation, with a visit of us at the address of the client where we will provide an estimate of the order, the visit of a specialist is free for each client. A contract is signed with each client, in which absolutely all the necessary information is indicated, as terms of work, cost of work, insurance payments, sizes of works and many other things, including the FIXED RATE for each contract.

GEOGRAPHY

Our georgraphy is CONSTANTLY EXPANDING throughout the United States of America, and today we work for our clients mostly - long distance moving, which is 80% of all orders and only 20% is local moving in Illinois, Wisconsin, Indiana.
