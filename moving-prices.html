Our prices

Local moving

This price includes all services and charges, such as packing furniture in special blankets, taking the furniture apart, assembling the furniture, lifting and loading items into the truck, professional placing and safe transportation of items and furniture in the truck, use of professional blankets, straps, duct tape, unloading the items at the delivery address, placing items as requested by the customer. Stairs inside the house or townhouse in the basement, and the stairs to the second or third floor are not paid additionally. Prices are quoted for the use of 1 box-truck 16ft and the services of 2 movers. There is no limit to the number of trips it takes to complete an order, and there is no mileage limit on orders within 100 miles of the loading address, anything over 100 miles from the loading address is classified as long distance moving. You can find fixed prices for long distance moves on our website, or you can call us to schedule a free appointment with a specialist.
